Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to make a statue?

Most statues are ready within 7–10 business days after we confirm your order. Rush orders can be completed in 4–5 days for an additional charge.

What kind of photo works best?

A clear, well-lit, front-facing photo works best. Higher resolution means finer details on your statue. We can work with multiple photos too!

Can I get a statue of a pet or cartoon character?

Absolutely! We can create statues of pets, fictional characters, anime figures, or any concept you have in mind.

Do you deliver outside Purnea?

Yes! We deliver across India. Local deliveries in Purnea are free, and pan-India shipping charges will be included in your quote.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

We share a 3D preview before printing. If you're not happy with the final product, we offer revisions and a satisfaction guarantee.
